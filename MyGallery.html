My Gallery

  • Percy Jackson and The Olympians by Rick Riordan

    • Percy Jackson & the Olympians is a series of five fantasy novels written by American author
    Rick Riordan, and the first book series in the Camp Half-Blood Chronicles. The novels are set
    in a world with the Greek gods in the 21st century, and follows the protagonist Percy Jackson,
    a young demigod who must prevent the Titans, led by Kronos (Cronus), from destroying the world.

  • Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

    • Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling.
    The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione
    Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and
    Wizardry.

  • The Selection Series by Kiera Cass

    • The Selection is a young adult novel by Kiera Cass first published on April 14, 2012 by
    HarperCollins. It is the first in a five-book series, followed by The Elite (2013),
    The One (2014), The Heir (2015) and The Crown (2016).

  • Paper Towns, Loooking for Alaska, The Fault in Our Stars and more by John Green

    • John Green is the #1 New York Times bestselling author of Looking for Alaska, An Abundance
    of Katherines, Paper Towns, The Fault in Our Stars, and Turtles All the Way Down.
